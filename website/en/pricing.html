Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any long-term contracts?

No. All our plans are month-to-month. You can cancel at any time with no penalties.

Is ad spend included?

No. The Pro and Enterprise plans include Google Ads and Meta Ads management, but the budget you spend on advertising is paid directly to Google/Meta. We manage and optimize how that budget is spent.

Who reviews the content before publishing?

All AI-generated content is reviewed by a human editor. Nothing gets published without approval.

Can I change plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do you accept payments in Mexican pesos?

Our prices are in US dollars but we accept payments with any international card. The approximate MXN exchange rate is listed as reference.
